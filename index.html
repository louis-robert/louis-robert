II. Niagara Falls

Niagara falls, one of the 7 wonders of the world. What more can I say ? It worths its wonders rank. It was just amazing. You arrive there, you see nothing. I asked myself "what did I paid for ??". Yes, a bit disapointed. But when at the top of it, it worths it. These are not the highest falls, but the most impressives ones, by far. A bottom less pit with rainbows.

III. Toronto's food

Just ate my best brunch at Mildred's Temple Kitchen. French croissant, with poached egg inside with a french bernaise sauce. And pancakes for sure ! A really good place to go to if you plan to go to Toronto ! Prices are quiete ok, around 22$CAD/person, so around 14 and 15 euros. That's THE place to be if you're an influencer or if you like good (and healthy) things.
